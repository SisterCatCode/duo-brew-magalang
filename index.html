Our Coffee and Milktea Journey

From bean to cup, we're dedicated to crafting exceptional coffee experiences.

Brewing Excellence Since 2024

Our business operates under a franchise model. I initially discovered it through advertisements on Facebook, which sparked my interest. After attending their online orientation, I was impressed by the concept and shared it with my spouse. We both saw its potential and decided to proceed with franchising. We officially acquired the franchise in October and successfully opened our business on November 11, 2024.
